# Ringtone sources and licenses

Attribution ledger for every shipped ringtone asset. Excerpts (`.caf`, mono IMA4,
≤29s, 1s fade tail unless the source is a short loop) are derived from the listed
source; `_full.m4a` files (stereo AAC 128k) are the uncut recordings. Keep this file
in sync with `RingtoneCatalog.swift` and the in-app Credits screen.

The repository's MIT license does not replace or override the licenses listed
here. Each recording remains available under its source license.

### Project-generated digital alarm tones

Bedside Quad and Classic Buzzer are simple synthesized waveforms made from
fixed-frequency chirps and buzzers. They are not AI-generated media or recordings
of third-party material. The project dedicates both tones to the public domain
under [CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

The shipped CAF files are mono, 44.1 kHz IMA4 transcodes of these source WAVs:
